Founding Story

The Student Developers Community (SDC) was established in 2022 at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST), founded by Mr. Chandrashekhar M — a SNIST alumnus from the 2021–2024 batch.

His vision was simple: create a space where students could grow technically through collaboration and hands-on learning, moving beyond classroom theory into real-world technology. Today, SDC has grown into a strong technical community uniting over 5000+ members with a growing national footprint under SDC INDIA.
